About

Profile

I work at the intersection of agricultural robotics, computer vision, and AI-driven sensing, focusing on building practical systems that work in real field conditions.

My research spans robotics for selective harvesting and mechanization, deep learning models for detection/segmentation, and spectroscopy-coupled AI for rapid trait prediction and germplasm screening. (You can edit this text anytime.)

Research

Focus areas

Robotics for harvesting & mechanization

Design, control, and validation of selective harvesting systems (end-effectors, kinematics, timing, field robustness).

Computer vision in agriculture

Detection/segmentation in clutter, occlusion, and variable illumination using CNNs and vision transformers.

Spectroscopy + AI

NIR/FTIR/HSI-driven trait prediction using chemometrics and deep learning for rapid, non-destructive screening.
