About

I work at the intersection of agricultural robotics, computer vision, and deep learning, focusing on practical sensing and automation solutions for agriculture and allied sciences. My doctoral work (IIT Kharagpur) developed a robotic arm for cotton boll harvesting.

Education
Ph.D. (2023), IIT Kharagpur — Robotic arm for cotton boll harvesting • M.Tech (2014), IIT Kharagpur — Farm Machinery & Power • B.Tech (2012), CAE Raichur — Agricultural Engineering
Research interests
  • Vision-based detection & segmentation for field crops
  • Robotic manipulation for selective harvesting
  • Deep learning for decision support and mobile/edge deployment
  • Sensors, embedded systems, and smart mechanization
